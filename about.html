About NJ Wholesale Embroidery

Crafting premium embroidered apparel since 2024

Our Story

NJ Wholesale Embroidery started in a small garage in New York with just one embroidery machine and a dream. We saw that most embroidered apparel on the market either had poor quality or sky-high prices. We wanted to change that.


Today, we're a team of passionate designers, machinists, and quality control experts who believe everyone deserves access to premium, beautifully embroidered clothing. Every piece that leaves our shop is made with care, precision, and attention to detail.


We're not just making clothes — we're creating wearable art that tells your story.
