Our Embroidery Process

From design to finished product

What is Embroidery?

Embroidery is the art of decorating fabric using needle and thread. Unlike screen printing, embroidery adds texture and dimension — giving your apparel a premium, lasting look.

How It Works

1

Design Creation

We start with your artwork. Our designers optimize each design for embroidery, converting images into digital embroidery files (called "tapes"). We adjust stitch density, underlay, and thread colors to ensure the design looks great on fabric.

2

Fabric Selection

We source premium fabrics that hold embroidery well. Cotton twill, canvas, and heavy fleece are ideal. We pre-treat fabrics to ensure smooth stitching and prevent puckering. Each garment is inspected before embroidery.

3

Digitizing

Using specialized software, we create a "run" file that tells the embroidery machine exactly where to place each stitch. This includes: stitch direction, density, pull compensation, and thread sequence.

4

Embroidery Machine Setup

Each garment is hooped and stabilized in the embroidery machine. We load the correct thread colors (up to 15 colors per design) and run a test stitch to ensure perfect placement before production.

5

Stitching

Our commercial embroidery machines stitch your design with precision. A typical design takes 15-30 minutes per garment. The machine uses high-quality polyester thread that's durable and fade-resistant.

6

Quality Control & Finishing

Every piece is inspected for loose threads, misaligned stitches, or fabric puckering. We trim thread ends, apply backing (if needed), and package your order with care.

Premium Embroidery

  • 3D puff texture
  • Durable & long-lasting
  • Vibrant thread colors
  • Eco-friendly materials
  • Professional finish

Cheaper Alternatives

  • Flat printed design (no texture)
  • Fades after washing
  • Cracks and peels over time
  • Lower quality threads
  • Feels cheap

Thread Quality

We use premium polyester thread that is:
• Fade-resistant (won't lose color in sunlight)
• Machine washable
• Soft to the touch
• Environmentally friendly

Stitch Count

Our standard embroidery uses 8,000-15,000 stitches per design, ensuring detailed artwork without compromising durability. For more complex designs, we go up to 25,000+ stitches.
